Metropolitan Area Networks (MANs) are networks that connect LANs together within a city. The main criteria for a MAN is that the connection between LANs is through a local exchange carrier (the local phone company). WANs (Wide Area Networks)

Wide Area Networks (WANs) connect LANs together between cities. The main difference between a MAN and a WAN is that the WAN uses Long Distance Carriers. Otherwise the same protocols and equipment are used as a MAN.
